FAQ's

1. What do I need to rent a car?
Typically, you need a valid driver's license, a credit card in the renter's name, and sometimes an additional form of identification.
2. What is the minimum age to rent a car?
The minimum age requirement varies by country and rental company, but it's usually around 21 years old. Drivers under 25 may incur a young driver surcharge.
3. Can I rent a car with a debit card?
Many car rental companies accept debit cards, but they may require additional forms of identification and a credit check. Credit cards are often preferred.
